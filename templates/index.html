Face Emotion Reader

Use your phone or laptop camera to read happy / sad expressions live in the browser.

Preparing face reader…

Open this page on your phone while your mobile browser and your computer are on the same Wi-Fi network.

Example address: http://<computer-ip>:5000

Allow camera permission, then hold the phone so your face is visible.

How it works

  • The browser captures live video from your front camera.
  • The page uses face-api.js to detect the face expression.
  • It classifies the face as Happy, Sad, or Neutral.

Built with Flask and face-api.js in the browser.
